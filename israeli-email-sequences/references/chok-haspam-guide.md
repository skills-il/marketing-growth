# Chok HaSpam (Israeli Anti-Spam Law) Guide

## Amendment 40 to the Communications Law (2008)
The Israeli anti-spam law requires **explicit prior consent (opt-in)** before sending commercial electronic messages (email, SMS, fax).

## Key Requirements
1. **Explicit consent**: Must be recorded and provable
2. **Sender identification**: Every message must clearly identify who sent it
3. **Unsubscribe mechanism**: Must be honored within 3 business days
4. **No pre-checked boxes**: Consent must be active, not passive

## Penalties
- Up to 50,000 NIS per violation (without proof of damage)
- Class action lawsuits are common and successful

## Exceptions (Consent Not Required)
- Existing customer relationship (purchased in last 12 months)
- Similar products/services to what customer already bought
- Must still include unsubscribe option

## Best Practices
- Double opt-in recommended
- Keep consent records with timestamp and source
- Honor unsubscribe within 24 hours (law says 3 days)
- Include physical address in footer
- Never buy email lists
- Separate transactional from marketing emails
