| Outlet | Language | Focus | Audience |
|--------|----------|-------|----------|
| Geektime | Hebrew | Tech/Startups | Developers, founders |
| Calcalist Tech | Hebrew | Business+Tech | Business leaders |
| The Marker | Hebrew | Business/Economy | Finance professionals |
| Globes | Hebrew | Business | Investors, executives |
| Ynet Tech | Hebrew | General tech | General public |
| CTech | English | Israeli tech | International audience |
| No Camels | English | Israeli innovation | Global tech community |
| Walla Tech | Hebrew | Consumer tech | Young adults |

## Content Formats That Work
1. Case studies with Israeli clients (highest B2B effectiveness)
2. Data-driven reports with Israeli market data
3. Hebrew how-to guides
4. Founder story features
5. Comparison articles with NIS pricing

## Distribution Channels
- **Google SEO**: Primary discovery channel
- **Facebook Groups**: Highest engagement for B2C
- **LinkedIn**: Best for B2B, Israeli tech ecosystem
- **WhatsApp**: Viral sharing, group-based
- **Telegram**: Developer communities
- **Email**: Chok HaSpam compliant newsletters
