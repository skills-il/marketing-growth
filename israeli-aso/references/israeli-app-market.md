# Israeli Mobile App Market

## Market Overview
- ~8.5M smartphone users (~85% penetration)
- Android: ~70%, iOS: ~30%
- Average apps per device: 40+
- App revenue per user: Higher than MENA average

## Top App Categories in Israel
| Category | Key Players | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Fintech | Bank Hapoalim, Leumi, bit, PayBox | Mobile banking very mature |
| Transport | Moovit, Gett, Yango | Moovit founded in Israel |
| Food Delivery | Wolt, 10bis, Cibus | Corporate meal cards huge |
| Shopping | Zap, KSP, iHerb | Price comparison culture |
| News | Ynet, Walla, Haaretz | Hebrew content essential |

## Apple App Store (Israel)
- Keyword field: 100 characters
- Title: 30 characters
- Subtitle: 30 characters
- Localization: he (Hebrew)
- Currency: ILS (₪)

## Google Play (Israel)
- Title: 30 characters
- Short description: 80 characters
- Full description: 4,000 characters
- Localization: iw (legacy code for Hebrew)
- Keywords extracted from description
