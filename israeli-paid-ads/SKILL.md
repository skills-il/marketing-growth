# Israeli Paid Ads

## Instructions

### Step 1: Choose Platform and Campaign Type

| Platform | Best For | Israeli Market Notes |
|----------|----------|---------------------|
| Google Ads (Search) | High-intent traffic, B2B, services | Hebrew keywords have less competition than English; avg CPC in Israel: 3-8 NIS for most verticals |
| Google Ads (Display) | Brand awareness, remarketing | Target Israeli news sites: Ynet, Walla, Mako, Calcalist |
| Meta (Facebook) | B2C, local businesses, ecommerce | ~6.5M Israeli users; Facebook groups are dominant discovery channel |
| Meta (Instagram) | Lifestyle, fashion, food, young demos | ~4M Israeli users; Stories and Reels outperform feed |
| LinkedIn Ads | B2B, SaaS, recruitment | Israel has one of highest LinkedIn penetration rates per capita |
| TikTok Ads | Gen Z, brand awareness, viral campaigns | Growing rapidly in Israel; Hebrew content outperforms English |

### Step 2: Hebrew Keyword Research (Search Ads)

**Keyword Strategy for Hebrew:**
- Hebrew is morphologically rich; one root can have many forms:
  - Root: ב.ט.ח (b.t.ch) generates: ביטוח, לבטח, מבוטח, ביטוחים
  - Target all common inflections as keywords
- Use Google Keyword Planner with location set to Israel
- Check both Hebrew and English variants (Israelis mix languages):
  - "ביטוח רכב" AND "car insurance israel"
  - "עורך דין" AND "lawyer tel aviv"

**Match Type Strategy for Hebrew:**
| Match Type | Hebrew Consideration | Example |
|------------|---------------------|---------|
| Broad | Captures morphological variants naturally | ביטוח (captures ביטוחים, לבטח) |
| Phrase | Use for multi-word Hebrew phrases | "ביטוח רכב זול" |
| Exact | Use for high-value exact terms | [ביטוח רכב השוואת מחירים] |

**Negative Keywords (Common Hebrew):**
- חינם (free), חינמי (free, adj.)
- בחינם (for free)
- לימודים (studies, unless education vertical)
- ויקיפדיה (wikipedia)
- מה זה (what is, unless top-funnel)

### Step 3: Write Hebrew Ad Copy

**Google Ads Hebrew Copy Rules:**
- Headlines: Up to 30 characters (Hebrew chars count same as English)
- Descriptions: Up to 90 characters
- Hebrew reads RTL; preview in actual ad format, not just text editor
- Use Hebrew numerals convention: ₪99 (shekel sign before number)

**Headline Formulas for Israeli Market:**
```
Formula 1 (Benefit): [תועלת] + [בישראל/בת"א/בירושלים]
Example: ביטוח רכב הזול ביותר בישראל

Formula 2 (Urgency): [פעולה] + עכשיו + [הנחה]
Example: הזמינו עכשיו וקבלו 20% הנחה

Formula 3 (Social Proof): [מספר] + לקוחות + [כבר/בחרו]
Example: 50,000 לקוחות כבר בחרו בנו

Formula 4 (Question): [שאלה רלוונטית]?
Example: מחפשים עורך דין לענייני נדל"ן?
```

**Israeli Ad Copy Best Practices:**
- Israelis respond well to direct, no-nonsense language
- Price transparency works better than in other markets
- Local trust signals: "חברה ישראלית" (Israeli company), city names
- Include phone number extensions (Israelis prefer calling)
- Use informal register (pniyah b'guf sheni, "you" form)

### Step 4: Israeli Audience Targeting

**Geographic Targeting:**
| Region | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Gush Dan (Tel Aviv metro) | Highest income, tech-savvy, ~40% of ad spend |
| Haifa and North | Industrial, multicultural audience |
| Jerusalem | Religious demographics, government |
| Be'er Sheva and South | Growing tech hub, lower CPCs |
| Peripheral cities | Lower competition, cost-effective for local businesses |

**Demographic Targeting for Israel:**
- Age: Military service at 18 shifts timelines (university at 23-24, first job at 26-27)
- Language: Target Hebrew primarily; Arabic for ~20% of population; Russian for ~15%
- Device: Mobile-first market (>70% of searches on mobile)
- Schedule: Avoid Friday afternoon through Saturday evening (Shabbat); peak Sunday-Thursday

**Custom Audiences:**
- Israeli tech workers: Target startup events (DLD Tel Aviv, Cybertech)
- Parents: Israeli school calendar (September start, summer July-August)
- Business owners: Target Israeli business publications readers

### Step 5: Israeli Ad Regulations Compliance

**Consumer Protection Law (Chok Haganat HaTzarchan, 1981) - Advertising Rules:**
- All prices must include VAT (17%) unless clearly marked otherwise
- "Sale" claims must show original price and discounted price
- Comparative advertising must be factual and verifiable
- Testimonials must be genuine and not misleading

**Digital Advertising Specific Rules:**
- Sponsored content must be clearly labeled: "תוכן ממומן" or "פרסומת"
- Influencer partnerships require disclosure per Israeli Advertising Council guidelines
- Health and financial claims have stricter requirements
- Real estate ads must include registration numbers
- Food ads must comply with "Healthy Food" labeling law

**Google Ads Israel-Specific Policies:**
- Pharmaceutical ads require Israeli Ministry of Health approval
- Financial services ads must include risk disclaimers
- Political ads have transparency requirements during election periods

### Step 6: Budget and Bidding Strategy

**Israeli Market Benchmarks (approximate ranges):**
| Vertical | Avg CPC (NIS) | Avg CTR | Avg Conv Rate |
|----------|--------------|---------|---------------|
| Legal | 15-40 | 2-4% | 3-5% |
| Insurance | 10-30 | 1.5-3% | 2-4% |
| Real Estate | 8-25 | 2-4% | 1-3% |
| Ecommerce | 2-8 | 3-6% | 2-5% |
| SaaS/Tech B2B | 5-20 | 2-4% | 1-3% |
| Education | 3-12 | 3-5% | 4-8% |
| Local Services | 3-10 | 4-7% | 5-10% |

**Bidding Recommendations:**
- Start with Manual CPC for first 2 weeks to gather data
- Switch to Target CPA once you have 30+ conversions
- Israeli market is smaller; use Maximize Conversions for smaller budgets
- Day-parting: Reduce bids on Friday afternoon and Saturday

## Troubleshooting

- **Issue**: Low CTR on Hebrew ads
  **Solution**: Check that ad preview shows correctly in RTL. Test informal vs. formal Hebrew register. Add price in NIS and local trust signals.

- **Issue**: High CPC compared to benchmarks
  **Solution**: Israeli market has seasonal spikes (pre-holidays, back to school in September). Check competitor density. Try exact match keywords and improve Quality Score with landing page relevance.

- **Issue**: Ads disapproved for Israeli regulations
  **Solution**: Ensure VAT-inclusive pricing, add required disclaimers for financial/health verticals, label sponsored content clearly. Check Google's Israel-specific advertising policies.
